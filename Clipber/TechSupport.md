# Clipber Technical Support

Clipber is a local-first macOS clipboard history and screenshot utility.

If you need help, please contact us by email:

shadowhounter@icloud.com

## Common Questions

### Clipboard history is not updating

Make sure Clipber is running in the macOS menu bar. If necessary, quit and reopen the app.

### Screenshot or OCR does not work

Open macOS System Settings and check the permissions related to Screen Recording and Accessibility. Grant Clipber the required permissions, then reopen the app.

### Where is my data stored?

Clipber stores clipboard history, screenshots, and settings locally on your Mac. Clipber does not upload your clipboard content or screenshots to a server.

## 常见问题

### 剪贴板历史没有更新

请确认 Clipber 正在 macOS 菜单栏中运行。如有需要，可以退出并重新打开 App。

### 截图或 OCR 无法使用

请打开 macOS 系统设置，检查屏幕录制和辅助功能相关权限。为 Clipber 授予所需权限后，重新打开 App。

### 数据保存在哪里？

Clipber 会将剪贴板历史、截图和设置保存在你的 Mac 本机。Clipber 不会把剪贴板内容或截图上传到服务器。
